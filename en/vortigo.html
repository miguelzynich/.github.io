About Vortigo

The company has been operating for over 10 years in the technology market, developing custom software solutions for sectors such as healthcare, finance, education, and services.

Objective

The main goal of this project was to simplify and make the user experience fluid and accessible.

Process

As this was a UI redesign project, I opted for a direct process focused on visual clarity. I started by creating low and high-fidelity prototypes to test different layout approaches. The delivery deadline was three weeks, which required agile and objective decisions. Throughout the process, I prioritized accessibility, applying principles from the WCAG guidelines and conducting color contrast and legibility tests to ensure an inclusive experience.

Grids

In this project, I used the Bootstrap 5 grid to structure the page more flexibly. This helped the elements fit well, making everything lighter, organized, and easy to navigate. The idea was to create a clean and intuitive look without complicating the user experience.
